# AnneseDanielle-web-project
## Final Project - Full website for GreenTech Solutions 
This project was a bit difficult to create, the background script and design was relatively easy but the thing I had the most trouble with was the content of the site. I tried to have a bit of fun with the "team" and the information on the site but for my first actual website, I am pretty proud of myself. This website showcases a simple home page, multiple buttons to navigate through a few pages. These pages are "Home", "About us", "For Individuals", "for Businesses", "Products", "Blog", "Site Navigation". There is a simple form to add a users email to the email list that runs through a check to ensure it is a valide email address. I put multiple free images, and Videos I also added my video walkthrough to the navigation buttons to access. The details of this site can be improved or changed to fit any needs that might arise such as new products being added or old products being removed. We could also update the "About Us" tab to match who is in current positions with the company. With that we could also swap out images and videos to better suit what the executives might want for the site. While there are some things that should remain the same it is relatively straight forward what can be updated.
