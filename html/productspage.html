PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

ENERGY-EFFICIENT DEVICES

One service we offer is energy-efficient devices. These devices are designed to reduce energy consumption and minimize environmental impact. They include energy-efficient appliances, LED lighting, and smart home systems that optimize energy use.

By using energy-efficient devices, businesses and individuals can significantly reduce their energy consumption, lower their carbon footprint, and save money on energy bills. These devices are an essential part of our sustainable technology solutions and can help create a more sustainable future.

When purchasing this service we have one of our experts come to your home or business to assess your current energy usage and make recommendations at varying price points so you can make the best decision for your home or business.

Once your package is selected we schedule an install where our team will come and install and test all devices to ensure they are working. We also have 24 hour customer service where we can send a technician out to assess or replace any devices not working properly.

Buyback Program

Our buyback program is designed to encourage businesses and individuals to upgrade to more sustainable technology by offering a trade-in option for their old devices. Customers can trade in their old energy-efficient devices, smart recycling systems, or carbon tracking software for a discount on new products.

By participating in our buyback program, customers can easily transition to more sustainable technology while also reducing electronic waste. This program helps promote a circular economy and encourages responsible consumption.

When customers trade in their old devices, we ensure that they are properly recycled or disposed of in an environmentally friendly manner. This program is part of our commitment to sustainability and our efforts to create a more sustainable future.
